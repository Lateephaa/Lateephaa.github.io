Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 4 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash rice in cold water and set aside.
  2. Blend the tomatoes, bell peppers, onion, and garlic in a blender until smooth.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pot and add the blended tomato mixture. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the tomato paste, paprika, thyme, and curry powder to the pot and stir to combine.
  5. Add the chicken stock and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the rice to the pot and stir to combine. Cover the pot and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.
  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let sit for 5-10 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
