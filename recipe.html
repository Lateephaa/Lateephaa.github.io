Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a one-pot dish made with rice, tomatoes, peppers, and onions. It's a staple in Nigerian cuisine and is usually served at parties and special occasions.

Pounded Yam

Pounded Yam is a starchy dish made by boiling yam and then pounding it into a smooth, dough-like consistency. It's usually served with a soup or stew.

Egusi Soup

Egusi Soup is a thick soup made with ground melon seeds and vegetables such as spinach or bitter leaf. It's often served with fufu, a starchy side dish made from cassava or yam.
